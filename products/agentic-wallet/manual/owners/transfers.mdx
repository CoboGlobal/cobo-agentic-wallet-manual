## Transfer tokens

You can initiate a token transfer directly from the Cobo Agentic Wallet app without going through your agent. This is useful for moving funds manually or topping up a wallet.

Open the app, select your wallet, tap **Transfer**, and fill in the destination address, token, and amount. Owner-initiated transfers bypass pact policies entirely — they execute directly under your authority.

## Receive funds

To receive tokens, you need to share your wallet address with the sender. In the Cobo Agentic Wallet app, tap **Receive** to see your wallet address and its QR code. The sender can scan the QR code directly or copy the address.

Make sure to share the correct address for the chain the sender is using — your wallet may have different addresses on different chains.
