---
title: "Pause or revoke agent access"
description: "How to immediately stop your agent from moving funds — temporarily or permanently."
---

## Pause your agent instantly

If you see suspicious activity or just want to stop your agent immediately, you can freeze all its activity in one action. While frozen, your agent cannot submit any transactions — all attempts are blocked automatically.

Open the Human App and tap **Freeze** in your agent's settings, or tell the AI assistant:

> "Pause my agent immediately."

## Unfreeze when you're ready

To restore access, tell the assistant:

> "Unfreeze my agent."

Your agent resumes operating within its existing rules immediately. No reconnection or reconfiguration is needed.

## Revoke access permanently

To end your agent's access entirely, tell the assistant:

> "Revoke my agent's access."

This permanently removes all access. If you want to use the same agent again later, you'll need to go through the authorization process again.

## What happens to pending transactions

When you freeze your agent:

- Any transactions already executing on-chain continue to completion — they cannot be stopped once they've been submitted to the network
- Any transactions waiting for processing are blocked immediately
- Any transactions waiting for your approval remain in your queue — you can still approve or reject them at any time
