The Cobo Agentic Wallet skill gives your agent the knowledge it needs to work with your wallet: how to create addresses, check balances, transfer tokens, call smart contracts, and operate within pacts and policy decisions correctly.

When you run `caw onboard`, your agent becomes the wallet's owner and can transact without restrictions. You can later pair the wallet with the Human App to take control yourself and set pacts and approval rules — but that step is optional and can be done any time.

<Steps>

<Step title="Install the skill">
The skill teaches your agent how to interact with your wallet. Run this in your terminal:

```bash
npx skills add CoboGlobal/cobo-agentic-wallet-python-sdk --skill cobo-agentic-wallet-dev --yes --global
```

This also installs the `caw` CLI automatically.
</Step>

<Step title="Run caw onboard">
```bash
caw onboard
```

No account or sign-up required. This command creates your wallet and starts the TSS node on your machine. Your agent is immediately the wallet owner — it can transact without any spending limits or approval requirements.
</Step>

<Step title="Create an address and deposit funds">
Ask your agent to create an address, then deposit funds to that address.

<Tip>You do not need to keep gas tokens in your wallet. Cobo Gasless covers on-chain transaction fees automatically.</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Ask your agent to transact">
Your agent can now start operating — transfers, contract calls, and more. As the wallet owner, it has no spending limits or approval requirements by default.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Pair with the Human App to take control

After your agent creates the wallet, you can pair it in the Human App to become the human controller. Once you pair:

- You set pacts — your agent becomes a delegate and operates within the boundaries you define
- For each new task, your agent submits a pact for your approval — intent, execution plan, permissions, policies, and completion conditions
- Operations above your approval threshold pause and wait for your approval in the Human App
- Your signing keys are stored on your device — Cobo cannot move your funds without your participation

Pairing is optional. Your agent can continue operating without it. But if you want personal control over your funds, pairing is the step that establishes it.
