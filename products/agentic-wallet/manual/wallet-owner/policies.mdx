---
title: "Set rules and limits"
description: "Control what your agent can spend, where it can send funds, and when transactions need your approval."
---

After you pair your wallet with the Human App, you set owner-side guardrails in the app. These controls work alongside your approved pacts: routine operations within the approved boundaries execute automatically; operations above your limits pause and wait for your approval.

<Info>Before you pair the wallet with the Human App, your agent is the owner and has no spending limits. Pairing is the step that puts you in control.</Info>

## What you can control

| Rule | What it does |
|---|---|
| Per-transaction limit | Your agent cannot send more than this amount in a single transaction |
| Daily limit | Your agent cannot spend more than this amount per day in total |
| Approval threshold | Transactions above this amount pause and wait for you to approve or reject |
| Allowed networks | Your agent can only transact on the chains you specify |
| Allowed addresses | Your agent can only send funds to addresses you've approved |

## Set rules using the AI assistant

In the Human App, use the AI assistant to set and update your rules in plain language. Examples:

- "Set a $100 per-transaction limit."
- "Require my approval for anything over $50."
- "Only allow transfers to this address: 0x1234..."
- "Limit daily spending to $500."
- "Only allow Base and Ethereum networks."

The assistant confirms every change before applying it. To review your current rules at any time, ask:

> "Show me my current rules."

## How rules work together

When your agent attempts a transaction, all your rules are checked against the active pact. If any rule would block it:

- If it exceeds a hard limit → the transaction is blocked automatically and your agent receives a clear explanation
- If it exceeds your approval threshold → the transaction pauses and waits for your review in the Human App

Your agent always receives a clear explanation of why a transaction was blocked so it can adjust and try again within your limits.
