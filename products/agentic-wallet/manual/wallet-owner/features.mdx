Cobo Agentic Wallet lets your AI agent create and operate a crypto wallet on your behalf. How much control you want is up to you.

When your agent first creates the wallet, it operates without restrictions — useful for testing and early development. When you claim the wallet in the Human App, you become the owner: you set the rules, and your agent operates within them.

## Two stages of control

| | Before claiming | After claiming |
|---|---|---|
| **Who decides the rules** | No rules — agent acts freely | You — through your Default Policy |
| **Approval** | None required | Over-limit operations pause for your approval |
| **Key management** | Agent machine + Cobo | Agent machine + Cobo, plus your phone + Cobo |
| **Best for** | Testing, development | Live funds, ongoing use |

## What you can do after claiming

| Feature | What it means for you |
|---|---|
| Set a Default Policy | Define per-transaction limits, daily budgets, and which addresses your agent can send to |
| Require approval | Operations above your threshold pause and wait for you in the Human App before executing |
| Review activity | See every transaction your agent attempted — completed, paused, or blocked — with timestamps |
| Stop your agent instantly | Freeze all agent activity in one action, with no delay |
| Back up your keys | Store a backup of your mobile key share in iCloud, Google Drive, or as an encrypted file export |

## What your agent can and cannot do

| Your agent can | Your agent cannot |
|---|---|
| Transfer tokens within your policy limits | Raise or modify your policy limits |
| Query your wallet balance and transaction history | Access wallets you have not connected it to |
| Interact with protocols within your approved rules | Approve its own requests on your behalf |
| Request your approval for larger operations | Execute transactions after you pause or revoke access |
| | Hold or reconstruct your private keys |
