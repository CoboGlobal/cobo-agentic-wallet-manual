## Check your balance

In the Human App, your current balance is shown on the wallet overview page. You can see balances broken down by token and by chain.

You can also ask the AI assistant:

> "What's my current wallet balance?"

The assistant shows your balances and any recent changes.

## Review transaction history

The activity log shows every operation your agent attempted, whether it was allowed, required your approval, or was blocked. Each entry shows:

- What was attempted (transfer to address, contract interaction)
- The amount and token
- The outcome (completed, awaiting approval, blocked)
- Timestamp

To filter by outcome, ask the assistant:

> "Show me all blocked transactions this week."

> "Show me transactions that needed my approval."

For a full breakdown of what each status means and how to act on transactions that need your attention, see [Handle Transactions](/products/agentic-wallet/manual/get-started/pending-ops).
