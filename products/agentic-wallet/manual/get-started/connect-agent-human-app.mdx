When your agent runs `caw onboard`, it creates a wallet and is immediately the owner with no restrictions. Pairing the agent with the Human App establishes you as the human controller: your signing keys are generated on your phone, your agent becomes a delegate operating through pacts, and over-limit operations pause for your approval in the Human App.

## What changes when you pair

| | Before pairing | After pairing |
|---|---|---|
| **Owner** | Your agent | You |
| **Authorization model** | No restrictions | Every new task requires a pact — a structured delegation agreement with intent, execution plan, policies, and completion conditions |
| **Spending limits** | None | Controlled by your approved pacts and owner-side guardrails |
| **Approval requirement** | None | New pacts and over-limit operations pause for your approval |
| **Signing keys** | Agent machine + Cobo (2-of-2) | Agent machine + Cobo (2-of-2) plus your phone + Cobo (2-of-2) |
| **Key recovery** | Via agent key share | Via your phone or device backup |

<Info>Pairing is optional and can be done at any time — even after your agent has been transacting for a while. Your wallet history and funds carry over.</Info>

## How it works

**Your key share never leaves your phone**

When you pair the wallet with the Human App, the app generates a cryptographic key share on your device and stores it locally. This share is never sent to Cobo or your agent. A second key group is created with your phone + Cobo — your agent's existing key group (agent machine + Cobo) remains in place for everyday signing. Either group can independently sign transactions.

**Over-limit operations require your approval**

Because your phone holds a key share, operations above your approval threshold pause and wait for you to approve from the Human App. Your approval is not just a software check — it requires your device to participate in the signing. There is no way to bypass this without your phone.

**Recovery without losing access**

If you lose your phone, you can recover using your device backup. Your agent's key group continues to function in the meantime.

<Steps>

<Step title="Download and sign in to the Human App">
Download the Human App on your phone and create an account or sign in.
</Step>

<Step title="Get the pairing code from your agent">
Ask your agent to generate a pairing code:

```bash
caw claim
```

The command outputs a pairing code in the format `CAW-XXXXX`. It is valid for 30 minutes. If it expires, run the command again to generate a new one.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter the pairing code in the Human App">
In the Human App, tap **Pair agent** and enter the pairing code. The app looks up your wallet and shows you a confirmation screen with the wallet ID, current balance, and creation date.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm and complete pairing">
Review the wallet details and tap **Confirm**. The app completes the pairing process, creates your agent's delegation, and generates your mobile key group. This takes about 30 seconds.
</Step>

<Step title="Set your owner guardrails">
Open the Human App and set the spending and approval controls that should apply to your agent's activity.

> "Set a $500 per-transaction limit and require my approval for anything over $200."

Your agent can now start operating within the pact and guardrails you approved — routine transactions execute automatically, and operations above your threshold pause for your approval in the Human App.

<Tip>You do not need to keep gas tokens in your wallet. Cobo Gasless covers on-chain transaction fees automatically.</Tip>
</Step>

</Steps>
