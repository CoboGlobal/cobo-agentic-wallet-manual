Cobo Agentic Wallet gives your agent real autonomy — and gives you real certainty. Every agent operates through a pact: a structured delegation agreement with intent, execution plan, policies, and completion conditions. The wallet enforces it automatically.

When your agent first creates the wallet, it operates without restrictions — useful for testing and early development. When you pair the wallet with the Human App, you become the human controller: for each task, you set a pact, and your agent operates within it.

## Two stages of control

| | Before pairing | After pairing |
|---|---|---|
| **Who decides the rules** | No rules — agent acts freely | You — through pacts and owner-side guardrails |
| **Approval** | None required | New pacts and over-limit operations pause for your approval |
| **Key management** | Agent machine + Cobo | Agent machine + Cobo, plus your phone + Cobo |
| **Best for** | Testing, development | Live funds, ongoing use |

## What you can do after pairing

| Feature | What it means for you |
|---|---|
| Set a pact for each task | Define intent, review the execution plan, set policies, and specify completion conditions — your agent operates within these boundaries and access revokes automatically when the pact ends |
| Set owner guardrails | Define spending limits, approval thresholds, and allowed destinations for ongoing oversight |
| Require approval | Operations above your threshold pause and wait for you in the Human App before executing |
| Review activity | See every transaction your agent attempted — completed, paused, or blocked — with timestamps |
| Stop your agent instantly | Freeze all agent activity in one action, with no delay |
| Back up your keys | Store a backup of your mobile key share in iCloud, Google Drive, or as an encrypted file export |

## What your agent can do within a pact

| Your agent can | The pact ensures it cannot |
|---|---|
| Transfer tokens within your pact's budget | Exceed spending limits or modify its own rules |
| Query your wallet balance and transaction history | Access wallets you have not connected it to |
| Interact with protocols within your approved scope | Approve its own requests on your behalf |
| Request your approval for larger operations | Continue operating after the pact expires or is revoked |
| Self-correct when a rule blocks an operation | Hold or reconstruct your private keys |
