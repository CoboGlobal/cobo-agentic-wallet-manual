This page covers Stage 3 of the [pact flow](/products/agentic-wallet/manual/get-started/pact-flow) — the moment your agent's proposed pact lands in front of you for review.

## What is a pact?

When your agent wants to take on a new task — for example, running a trading strategy or managing a payroll schedule — it submits a pact: a structured delegation agreement with intent, execution plan, policies, and completion conditions. Once you approve, your agent operates within those exact boundaries, and its access revokes automatically when the pact ends. You review and decide: approve, reject, or adjust the terms before approving.

<Info>Pacts only require your approval after you have paired the wallet with the Human App. Before pairing, your agent is the owner and can operate without approval.</Info>

## What to review

A pact shows you:

- **Intent** — the job the agent is being authorized to carry out
- **Execution plan** — a reviewable plan for how the agent intends to carry out that job
- **Policies** — the budgets, approvals, allowlists, chain/token/contract constraints, and other guardrails it's proposing
- **Completion conditions** — what triggers automatic completion and revocation (time elapsed, budget exhausted, transaction count reached, job done)

## Approve, reject, or adjust

You have three options:

- **Approve** — your agent gets access to proceed within the pact's boundaries
- **Reject** — your agent gets no access; nothing changes
- **Adjust before approving** — change the policies or completion conditions before granting access; your agent operates under your revised terms, not its original proposal

Every pact has an endgame. Active pacts complete automatically when a completion condition is met — time elapsed, budget exhausted, or job done — no zombie permissions, no lingering access. You can also revoke an active pact at any time from the same screen.

## Where to find pending pacts

Open the Human App and go to **Pending Approvals**. You'll see a list of pacts waiting for your decision, each with a timestamp and the agent's stated intent.

You can also ask the AI assistant:

> "Do I have any pending pacts?"
