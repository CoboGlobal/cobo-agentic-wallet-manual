## What is an authorization request?

When your agent wants to take on a new task — for example, running a trading strategy or managing a payroll schedule — it submits an authorization request explaining what it wants to do, for how long, and within what limits. It is a structured agreement, not just a permission: once you approve, your agent operates within those exact boundaries, and its access expires automatically when the window ends. You review and decide: approve, reject, or adjust the terms before approving.

<Info>Authorization requests only require your approval after you have claimed the wallet. Before claiming, your agent is the owner and can operate without approval.</Info>

## What to review

An authorization request shows you:

- **What your agent wants to do** — a plain-language description of the task
- **What it plans to do** — a detailed breakdown of its strategy and steps
- **What permissions it's requesting** — for example, transfer tokens or interact with specific contracts
- **Spending limits** — the per-transaction and daily budgets it's proposing
- **Duration** — how long it wants this authorization to last; access expires automatically when that window closes, with no action needed from you

## Approve, reject, or adjust

You have three options:

- **Approve** — your agent gets access to proceed with the task within the limits shown
- **Reject** — your agent gets no access; nothing changes
- **Adjust before approving** — change the spending limits or duration before granting access; your agent operates under your revised terms, not its original proposal

Active authorizations also expire automatically when their duration ends or their spending budget is exhausted — no zombie permissions, no lingering access. You can also revoke an active authorization at any time from the same screen.

## Where to find pending requests

Open the Human App and go to **Pending Approvals**. You'll see a list of authorization requests waiting for your decision, each with a timestamp and the agent's stated intent.

You can also ask the AI assistant:

> "Do I have any pending authorization requests?"
