The Cobo Agentic Wallet skill gives your agent the knowledge it needs to work with your wallet: how to create addresses, check balances, transfer tokens, call smart contracts, and handle authorization requests and policy decisions correctly.

When you run `caw onboard`, your agent becomes the wallet's owner and can transact without restrictions. You can later claim the wallet in the Human App to take ownership yourself and set approval rules — but that step is optional and can be done any time.

<Steps>

<Step title="Install the skill">
The skill teaches your agent how to interact with your wallet. Run this in your terminal:

```bash
npx skills add CoboGlobal/cobo-agentic-wallet-python-sdk --skill cobo-agentic-wallet-dev --yes --global
```

This also installs the `caw` CLI automatically.
</Step>

<Step title="Run caw onboard">
```bash
caw onboard
```

No account or sign-up required. This command creates your wallet and starts the TSS node on your machine. Your agent is immediately the wallet owner — it can transact without any spending limits or approval requirements.
</Step>

<Step title="Create an address and deposit funds">
Ask your agent to create an address, then deposit funds to that address.

<Tip>You do not need to keep gas tokens in your wallet. Cobo Gasless covers on-chain transaction fees automatically.</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Ask your agent to transact">
Your agent can now start operating — transfers, contract calls, and more. As the wallet owner, it has no spending limits or approval requirements by default.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Claim the wallet to take ownership

After your agent creates the wallet, you can claim it in the Human App to become the owner yourself. Once you claim:

- You set the rules — your agent becomes a delegate and can only act within your Default Policy
- Operations above your policy limits pause and wait for your approval in the Human App
- Your signing keys are stored on your device — Cobo cannot move your funds without your participation

Claiming is optional. Your agent can continue operating without it. But if you want personal control over your funds, claiming is the step that establishes it.
