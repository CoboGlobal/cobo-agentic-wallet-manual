title: "Guide Your Users Through Onboarding"
description: "How to walk your users through connecting an agent to Cobo Agentic Wallet using the Web Console or Human App."
---

This page is for developers building products where their users are the wallet owners. It explains how to instruct your users to complete onboarding on their end — you don't need to build a custom onboarding UI.

## The owner's onboarding flow

Your users complete onboarding on the Cobo side. The flow:

1. Your user signs in to the Web Console (or downloads the Human App)
2. They generate a pairing code and setup instruction
3. They share the setup instruction with your agent (paste it into the agent's interface)
4. Your agent runs the instruction, which internally calls `caw onboard provision --token <code>`
5. Your agent is registered and ready to operate their wallet

## What to tell your users (Web Console path)

Give your users these instructions:

1. Go to the Web Console and sign in
2. Click **Connect Agent**
3. Copy the setup instruction shown on screen
4. Paste it into [your product's] chat input
5. Wait for your agent to confirm setup is complete (about 1–3 minutes)
6. Set your spending rules using the AI assistant: "Set a $100 daily limit and require approval above $50"

<Tip>
Customize these steps for your product's terminology. "Chat input" becomes whatever your interface calls it — message box, command bar, or agent console.
</Tip>

## What to tell your users (Human App path)

Give your users these instructions:

1. Download the Human App and sign in
2. Create your wallet in the app (follow the in-app prompts)
3. Tap **Connect Agent** to generate a pairing code
4. Copy the setup instruction and paste it into [your product's] chat input
5. Wait for your agent to confirm setup is complete (about 30 seconds)
6. In the Human App, authorize your agent: tell the assistant "Authorize my agent to use my wallet"

## After your user completes onboarding

Once setup is done, your agent will have:

- An API key bound to the owner's account
- Access to the wallet within whatever delegation was created during onboarding

At this point, your agent can call `caw pact submit` to request additional scoped authorization for specific tasks, which the owner reviews and approves in the Web Console or Human App.

## Directing users to policy setup

After onboarding, remind your users:

> "Before your agent starts, set the spending limits that work for you. In the Web Console, tell the assistant: 'Set a $100 daily limit and require my approval for anything over $50.'"

This ensures the policy engine has rules in place before your agent begins operating.
